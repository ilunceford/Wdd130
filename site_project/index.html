Historical Background About The Grand Canyon

To begin I chose to put Grand Canyon first because it is my favorite national park of the three I will be talking about. As a little background about the Grand Canyon, It was officially founded in 1919 but has been in formation for about 5 million years. The canyon was formed by the Colorado River which carved its way through rock still even today. The width of the Grand Canyon is massive with some parts being as wide as 18 miles. The full-length of the Grand Canyon is about 227 miles in length and is the second most visited national park in all of the United States.

My Experience At Grand Canyon National Park

The last time I visited I was with my family and we were able to hike around 8 miles of the South Rim Trail. We started our trip by driving for four and a half hours until we arrived and got in line to board a shuttle. I mentioned earlier that a lot of people visit it and the lines are very long. Once we got on the shuttle we drove to the trailhead where we hiked along the southern rim trail. The trail was beautiful the whole way because you had a glorious view of the Grand Canyon the entire way.
